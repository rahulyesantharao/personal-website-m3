About

Hi! I’m Rahul. I'm a senior/first-semester MEng student at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology, studying Course 6-2 (Electrical Engineering and Computer Science). During my time at MIT, I have directed DevOps for HackMIT and helped run the MIT IEEE/ACM student chapter.

Research

  • From 2017-2018, I worked in the Robot Locomotion Group with Professor Russ Tedrake. I worked on constrained footstep planning through the use of mixed-integer convex optimization, implementing a footstep optimizer with the Drake framework here.
  • I am currently doing research with Professors Tim Kraska and Malte Schwarzkopf, working on Tuplex.
  • I am also currently working on a SuperUROP under the MIT EECS Undergraduate Research and Innovation Scholar grant.
